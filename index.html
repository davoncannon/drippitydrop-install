💧 Drippity Drop

LAN file transfer between this device and your Windows PC. No cloud, no App Store.

Install Drippity Drop

Open this page in Safari on the iPhone or iPad you registered (ad-hoc builds only install on devices whose UDID is in the provisioning profile).

After tapping Install: if iOS says the app is from an untrusted developer, go to Settings › General › VPN & Device Management and trust your developer certificate.

This page and manifest.plist must be served over HTTPS with a certificate iOS trusts (a real one — Let's Encrypt is fine; self-signed is not).
